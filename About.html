DK Vinyl Studio Story


Hi, I am Kalaya, main person behind the DK Vinyl Studio and my husband helps me with all deliveries. Sometimes our little ones, Lilly and Ethan, help me with packing orders too. We are from a small town in West Yorkshire called Mirfield. We love to create T-shirts, Canvas Bags, Mugs and Hoodies using Heat Transfer Vinyl and Sublimation. We also love to create and design custom orders and had very high satisfaction from our customers. If you’re looking for something different than designs on high street for you or your loved ones, you’ve come to the right place. You will be guaranteed for best customer service from us and also quality products. Every product we made has all love and attention.



Frequently asked questions

Wholesale availability

If you do want different size that are not on the drop down list, feel free to check with us. We can check the stock at wholesaler and usually takes 3 working days to arrive from them.

Care instructions

We use quality PU Vinyl and heat pressed according to the instruction which will keep the quality of Vinyls decoration on t-shirts. You also need to follow the care instruction when you wash the garments which we provide you with the products.

Sizing details

All sizing are Unisex sizing so loose fit. Broadly speaking Men sizes go with Small, Medium or Large etcand Ladies sizes usually go with UK 8-10(Small), 10-12 (Medium), 12-14 (Large) and 14-16(XL).

Custom and personalised orders

If you do want to order a custom T-shirt, we will always get a proof from you first for the design before printing to make sure you are happy with the design. We will work with you on the design you want and guarantee 100% corporation. We are happy to accept wholesale order with discount rate too. Please contact us to discuss.

T-Shirt & Hoodies Brands

We use Gildan Soft-Style as a main brand but we use Bella and Canvas brands as a secondary source. We have tested these t-shirt brand and happy with the quality and softness of the garments. For hoodies and sweatshirts, we use Gildan 18000 and 18500. OEKO-TEX® CONFIDENCE IN TEXTILES STANDARD 100 FAIR LABOR ASSOCIATION

Delivery Time

We set out maximum processing time as 2-3 days plus delivery time. But usually we send out the items next day. If anything different from that due to unforseen stock issues, adverse weather condition etc, we will inform you. Hoodies and sweatshirts may take a little longer to dispatch. Please see current time on delivery profile. You can now upgrade to Royal Mail Tracked 24 but it will depend on the time of purchase to get posted the day after order has been processed.

Discount Code

When we have seasonal sale on across the store and you also have a discount code like Thank You discount etc, you can only use one discount at a time. Etsy will know which code has most discount available to you and will apply to your basket that will save you most money.
